About Telcontar

An operator searching for a business to steward—patient, prepared, and all-in.

Drew Nauman founded Telcontar LLC drawing on a diverse foundation in electrical and construction work, legal and financial operations, and modern technology. His experience managing teams, coordinating multi-step projects, and improving workflows across different industries informs a practical, systems-oriented approach to leadership. Drew’s background gives him a ground-level understanding of how strong systems, clear communication, and consistent execution create value in small businesses. Telcontar LLC represents his progression toward acquiring and operating a business with disciplined management and long-term operational excellence.
